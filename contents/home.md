Dr. Hua Zhang is a Professor in the School of Science, Department of Chemistry at Tianjin University, specializing in analytical chemistry. He earned his PhD from Jilin University in 2019 and subsequently completed postdoctoral training at the University of Wisconsin–Madison, School of Pharmacy from 2019 to 2025. His research focuses on mass spectrometry imaging and mass spectrometry–based multi-omics analysis. He has established a suite of highly sensitive, ultrahigh-spatial-resolution mass spectrometry imaging methodologies (tissue expansion mass spectrometry imaging, TEMI), together with innovative ambient ionization in situ mass spectrometry analysis approaches, to enable single-cell–level spatial multi-omics investigations of clinical tissues and rapid analysis of complex biospecimens. To date, he has published more than 60 peer-reviewed articles, including 24 as first or corresponding author in leading journals such as Nature Methods, Nature Communications, Chemical Science, and Analytical Chemistry.
